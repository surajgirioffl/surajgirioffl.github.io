02

Agreement & Kickoff

We agree on scope, milestones, and payment. I begin development immediately and share progress through GitHub or direct file delivery.

I build iteratively with regular check-ins. You're never left wondering what's happening — weekly (or more frequent) updates keep you in the loop.

Delivery & Support

Final delivery with documentation, deployment (if needed), and a handover walkthrough. Post-delivery support included for any issues.
